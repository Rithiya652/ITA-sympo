△ Bid or Out

02:00 – 03:30

Team Size: 4 members

Description

Theme: IPL Auction × Technical Quiz × Survival Challenge

Bid or Out is not your normal quiz — it's a survival auction of knowledge! 10 teams will face off in 3 thrilling rounds where every answer counts. From technical quizzing to knowledge auctions, participants must think fast, explain smarter, and outbid their rivals. Only the team that masters knowledge and strategy will claim victory!

"Bid Your Knowledge. Win Your Survival." | "In this game, knowledge is currency." | "Think Fast. Speak Smart. Survive Hard."

Rounds

  • Round 1: Team Draft (Tech Quiz Round)
    A rapid-fire technical quiz decides how teams are formed and who enters the arena.
    Knowledge is your draft power. Knowledge-based IPL teams will be provided.
  • Round 2: The Knowledge Auction
    "If you want players, we auction like IPL"
    Example: If the topic is Data Structures, participants must relate concepts (Trees, Stacks, Queues, Linked Lists).
    The more correct and detailed concepts a team gives, the stronger their bid.
    Every team will bid over bid one by one. The last team standing wins the player.
    This round decides who survives the auction!
  • Round 3: The Final Showdown (Special Quiz)
    The top qualifying teams face a high-stakes technical quiz.
    The toughest questions decide the ultimate winner.

□ The Hidden Marble

02:00 – 03:30

Team Size: 2 members

Description

A thrilling treasure hunt with tech twists! Navigate the campus, solve coding challenges, and follow the trail of clues. Teams must combine aptitude, debugging skills, logical reasoning, and exploration to find the hidden treasure.

Rounds

  • Round 1: Aptitude & Logical Quest
    Round 1 includes 20 aptitude, reasoning, and logic questions.
    Players first attempt 10 questions within a time limit.
    At least 8 correct answers unlock the next 10 questions.
    Difficulty increases gradually in the second set.
    Builds quick thinking, accuracy, and analytical skills.
    Serves as a warm-up for the technical stages of the treasure hunt.
  • Round 2: Debug to Location
    Round 2 begins the real treasure hunt adventure.
    Participants receive C programs with logical or syntax errors to debug.
    Each corrected code reveals a clue, keyword, or hint.
    Clues guide players to the first physical location of the trail.
    At each location, participants scan a QR code for the next clue.
    Following the chain of clues leads to the final treasure spot.
    Combines coding, teamwork, logical reasoning, and exploration.

Venue: UG1 Lab, IT Dept

Time: 2:00 - 3:30 PM

Note: One registration per team
